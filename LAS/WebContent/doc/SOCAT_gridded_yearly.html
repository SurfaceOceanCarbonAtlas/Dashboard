Surface Ocean Carbon Atlas (SOCAT) Gridded Yearly Dataset

References may be found at http://www.socat.info/

Gridded (binned) SOCAT observations with a spatial grid of 1x1 degree and yearly in time.

A dataset of yearly data is computed from the gridded 1-degree, 1-month dataset, so that the data from the start to the end of each year are combined as described below.


Variable Definition
Number of cruises Counts cruises which returned any data in the month and grid cell. The cruise counts within each year are summed.
Number of obs Counts all observations in the month and grid cell. The observation counts within each year are summed.
fco2 mean - unweighted all obs Mean of all observations from all cruises. The means for the months within each year are averaged. They are not re-weighted for the varying lengths of the months.
fco2 mean - per cruise weighted The weighted cruise means for the months within each year is averaged.
fco2 max The maximum for each year is used.
fco2 min The minimum for for each year is used.
ICOADS Air Temperature Monthly Air Temperature from ICOADS, averaged yearly
ICOADS Sea Surface Temperature Monthly Sea Surface Temperature from ICOADS, averaged yearly
ICOADS Sea Level Pressure Monthly Sea Level Pressure from ICOADS, averaged yearly
ICOADS Wind Speed Monthly Wind Speed from ICOADS, averaged yearly
