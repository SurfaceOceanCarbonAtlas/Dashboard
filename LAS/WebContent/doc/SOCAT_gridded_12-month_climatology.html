Surface Ocean Carbon Atlas (SOCAT) Gridded Climatology Dataset

References at http://www.socat.info/

Gridded (binned) SOCAT observations with a spatial grid of 1x1 degree, 12-month climatology.

A monthly climatology is computed from the gridded 1-degree, 1-month dataset, so that the data in each January for all years are combined, then all data in each February, etc. All available months are weighted equally.

NO INTERPOLATION WAS PERFORMED. SIGNIFICANT BIASES ARE PRESENT IN THESE GRIDDED RESULTS DUE TO THE ARBITRARY AND SPARSE LOCATIONS OF DATA VALUES IN BOTH SPACE AND TIME.


Variable Definition
Number of cruises Counts cruises which returned any data in the month and grid cell. The cruise counts for all years are summed.
Number of unique months in climatological average Counts the number of months that contributed to the climatological average for a grid cell and month-of-year.
Number of obs Counts all observations in the month and grid cell. The observation counts for all years are summed.
fco2 mean - unweighted all obs Mean of all observations from all cruises for the month and grid cell. The means for the month for all years from the gridded dataset are averaged.
fco2 mean - per cruise weighted The weighted cruise means for the given month in all years is averaged.
fco2 max The maximum for the given month over all years is used
fco2 min The minimum for the given month over all years is used
ICOADS Air Temperature Monthly Air Temperature from ICOADS, averaged for monthly climatology
ICOADS Sea Surface Temperature Monthly Sea Surface Temperature from ICOADS, averaged for monthly climatology
ICOADS Sea Level Pressure Monthly Sea Level Pressure from ICOADS, averaged for monthly climatology
ICOADS Wind Speed Monthly Wind Speed from ICOADS, averaged for monthly climatology
