Surface Ocean Carbon Atlas (SOCAT)

References may be found at http://www.socat.info/

Gridded (binned) SOCAT observations with a spatial grid of 1x1 degree and monthly in time.

The gridded fields are computed using only SOCAT cruises with QC flags of A through D and SOCAT data points flagged with WOCE flag values of 2. The grid is monthly in time and 1-degree in longitude and latitude, with the grid defined as for the World Ocean Atlas datasets, centered at half-degrees.

NO INTERPOLATION WAS PERFORMED. SIGNIFICANT BIASES ARE PRESENT IN THESE GRIDDED RESULTS DUE TO THE ARBITRARY AND SPARSE LOCATIONS OF DATA VALUES IN BOTH SPACE AND TIME.


Variables:
Variable Definition
Number of cruises Number of cruises containing observations in the grid cell
Number of obs Total number of observations in the grid cell
fco2 mean - unweighted all obs Arithmetic mean of all "fco2 recomputed" values found in the grid cell
fco2 mean - per cruise weighted Mean of "fco2 recomputed" computed by calculating the arithmetic mean value for each cruise passing through the cell and then averaging these cruises
fco2 max Maximum value of "fco2 recomputed" observed in the grid cell
fco2 min Minimum value of "fco2 recomputed" observed in the grid cell
fco2 std dev - unweighted all obs The standard deviation of "fco2 recomputed" computed from the unweighted mean
fco2 std dev - per cruise weighted A weighted standard deviation of "fco2 recomputed" computed to account for the differing variance estimates for each cruise passing through the cell. The statistical technique is described at http://wapedia.mobi/en/Weighted_mean#7.
Latitude average offset from cruise cell center The arithmetic average of latitude offsets from the grid cell center for all observations in the grid cell. The value of this offset can vary from -0.5 to 0.5. A value of zero indicates that the computed fco2 mean values are representative of the grid cell center position.
Longitude average offset from cruise cell center The arithmetic average of longitude offsets from the grid cell center for all observations in the grid cell. The value of this offset can vary from -0.5 to 0.5. A value of zero indicates that the computed fco2 mean values are representative of the grid cell center position.
