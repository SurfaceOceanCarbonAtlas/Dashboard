Surface Ocean Carbon Atlas (SOCAT) Gridded Decadal Dataset

References may be found at http://www.socat.info/

Gridded (binned) SOCAT observations with a spatial grid of 1x1 degree and decadal in time.

A dataset of decadal data is computed from the gridded 1-degree, 1-month dataset, so that the available data from the start to the end of each decade are combined. Months are weighted slightly unqually - based on the number of days in that month.

The first decade is 1-Jan-1970 through 31-Dec-1979; the last decade is generally a partial decade.

NO INTERPOLATION WAS PERFORMED. SIGNIFICANT BIASES ARE PRESENT IN THESE GRIDDED RESULTS DUE TO THE ARBITRARY AND SPARSE LOCATIONS OF DATA VALUES IN BOTH SPACE AND TIME.


Variable Definition
Number of cruises Counts cruises which returned any data in the month and grid cell. The cruise counts within each decade are summed.
Number of obs Counts all observations in the month and grid cell. The observation counts within each decade are summed.
fco2 mean - unweighted all obs Mean of all observations from all cruises. The means for the months within each decade are averaged.
fco2 mean - per cruise weighted The weighted cruise means for the months within each decade is averaged.
fco2 max The maximum for each decade is used.
fco2 min The minimum for for each decade is used.
ICOADS Air Temperature Monthly Air Temperature from ICOADS, averaged to decadal
ICOADS Sea Surface Temperature Monthly Sea Surface Temperature from ICOADS, averaged to decadal
ICOADS Sea Level Pressure Monthly Sea Level Pressure from ICOADS, averaged to decadal
ICOADS Wind Speed Monthly Wind Speed from ICOADS, averaged to decadal
